Steak Donburi Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious steak donburi.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Egg yolks
  • Rice
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Onions
  • Thai chili peppers
  • Bonito flakes
  • Wasabi

Instructions

  1. Season the steak
  2. Wash and cook the rice
  3. Prepare the garnish and sauce
  4. Cook the steak
  5. Cook the sauce
  6. Assemble the rice bowl
  7. Prepare the egg yolk
  8. Garnish and serve
