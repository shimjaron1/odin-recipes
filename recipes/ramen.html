Ramen Recipe

Description

Simple and delicious ramen recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodle pack
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Broth
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms
  • Veggies like carrots or kale
  • Favorite toppings (e.g. panko, egg, chili oil, etc.

Instructions

  1. Stir-fry the aromatics
  2. Make your broth
  3. Add noodles
  4. Add vegetables
  5. Top it off
